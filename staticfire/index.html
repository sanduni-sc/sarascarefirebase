About Us

Building a better future together Forever

SarasCare is a not-for-profit organisation that supports local communities to survive and thrive in tough times

  • More than support: We offer resources, skills development, and opportunities for lasting independence.
  • Beyond immediate needs: We build the foundation for a brighter future through education, job training, and community initiatives.
  • Hand in hand: We work collaboratively with volunteers, businesses, and other organisations to amplify our impact.

How can you help us?

Donate

Make a difference today by contributing to our cause. Your generous donation helps us create positive impact and change lives.

Fundraise

Participate

Be an active force for good – volunteer, attend events, and engage in our community initiatives. Your involvement is key to building a brighter future together.

Contribute
