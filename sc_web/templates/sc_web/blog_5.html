Collaborating With The Awaiters Community

By Anish Baniya

SarasCare working with The Awaiters community group in Tarniet. We were able to get hygiene and cleaning products to the community during the Covid-19 lockdowns. Community partnerships are vital to reach the community. Shown here is Gautam from SarasCare and the president of The Awaiters community Syed Kamran.

The only way to pull ourself out of this pandemic is by helping each other and giving back to the community that really needs it.

Gautam Gupta

In these challenging times, the importance of community partnerships and collective efforts cannot be overstated. Collaborative initiatives, such as the one between SarasCare and The Awaiters community group in Tarniet, demonstrate the positive impact that can be achieved when individuals and organizations come together. As we navigate the complexities of the Covid-19 pandemic, extending a helping hand to those in need becomes not only a responsibility but also a powerful means of pulling ourselves out of this crisis.

The heartwarming collaboration, as portrayed by Gautam from SarasCare and the president of The Awaiters community, Syed Kamran, exemplifies the spirit of unity and shared commitment to supporting communities in distress. By working hand in hand, we can contribute to a brighter, more resilient future for everyone.
